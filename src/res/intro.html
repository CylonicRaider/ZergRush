Zerg Rush

Evil Zergs are attacking your base! Defend it at any cost!

Your goal is to have yourself (the green triangle) and your base (the blue circle) survive for the longest amount of time in spite of the onslaught of the Zergs (red squares).

You (and the base) lose health by battling (via touching) Zergs, who lose health from the battle as well. You can restore your health by touching the base; the base itself does not regenerate.

Use the arrow keys to move around the game area. Further key bindings are listed below:

F1  —  View this message (not during game).
Escape  —  Quit (at any time).
